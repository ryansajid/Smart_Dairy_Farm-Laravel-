### Bangladesh Dairy Market

The Bangladesh dairy market presents significant growth potential:

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Market Size (2024)** | $4.6 Billion USD |
| **Projected Size (2032)** | $6.5 Billion USD |
| **Annual Growth Rate** | 9.04% (2024-2029) |
| **National Milk Production** | 10.79 million tonnes |
| **Global Ranking** | 5th in Asia, 19th worldwide |
| **Demand-Supply Gap** | 56.5% deficit |

### The Opportunity

- **Demand**: 14.02 Million MT annually
- **Production**: 6.09 Million MT (43.5% of demand)
- **Deficit**: 7.93 Million MT (56.5% gap)
- **Import Bill**: $93.4+ million annually

*Smart Dairy is positioned to capture this growing market with premium, technology-enabled dairy products.*
