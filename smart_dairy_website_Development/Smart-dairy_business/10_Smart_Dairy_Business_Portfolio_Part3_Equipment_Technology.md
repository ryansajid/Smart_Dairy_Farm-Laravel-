# Smart Dairy Ltd. - Comprehensive Business Portfolio

## Part 3: Dairy Equipment & Technology Business Opportunities

---

## Executive Summary

The global dairy equipment market is valued at $25 billion (2024) and growing at 5% CAGR. Bangladesh's dairy modernization presents a significant opportunity for Smart Dairy to leverage its technology expertise (from parent company Smart Group) to become a leading supplier of dairy equipment, machinery, and smart farming solutions.

**Market Drivers:**
- Increasing automation in Bangladesh dairy farms
- Labor cost rising (need for mechanization)
- Quality and food safety requirements
- Government subsidies for dairy equipment
- Growing commercial dairy farms
