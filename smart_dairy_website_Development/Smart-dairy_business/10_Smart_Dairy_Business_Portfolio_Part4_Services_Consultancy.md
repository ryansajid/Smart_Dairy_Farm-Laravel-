# Smart Dairy Ltd. - Comprehensive Business Portfolio

## Part 4: Dairy Services & Consultancy Business Opportunities

---

## Executive Summary

Beyond products and equipment, services represent a high-margin, recurring revenue opportunity for Smart Dairy. Leveraging its expertise in modern dairy farming, Smart Dairy can offer comprehensive consultancy, training, and support services to the growing dairy farming community in Bangladesh.

**Service Market Drivers:**
- 79,900+ registered dairy farms in Bangladesh (growing)
- 80% of farmers lack formal training
- Government push for dairy sector modernization
- Financial institutions require farm assessments for loans
- Growing demand for organic/sustainable farming practices
