## Strategic Recommendations

### Phase 1: Foundation (Year 1)
**Focus:** Build on existing capabilities

**Priorities:**
1. Expand pasteurized milk product line
2. Launch AI services with 10-20 technicians
3. Start dairy equipment distribution
4. Begin Smart Dairy Academy
5. Establish mobile veterinary service

**Investment Required:** BDT 5-8 Cr

### Phase 2: Expansion (Year 2)
**Focus:** Scale successful initiatives

**Priorities:**
1. Enter value-added dairy products (yogurt, butter, ghee)
2. Launch livestock trading platform
3. Expand equipment portfolio
4. Scale training programs
5. Develop export capabilities

**Investment Required:** BDT 10-15 Cr

### Phase 3: Ecosystem Leadership (Year 3)
**Focus:** Complete dairy ecosystem

**Priorities:**
1. Full dairy product portfolio including cheese
2. Comprehensive genetics services (ET)
3. Smart farming solutions
4. National training leadership
5. International partnerships

**Investment Required:** BDT 15-25 Cr
