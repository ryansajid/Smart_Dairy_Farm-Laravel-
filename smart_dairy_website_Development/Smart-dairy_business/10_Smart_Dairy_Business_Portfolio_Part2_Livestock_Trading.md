# Smart Dairy Ltd. - Comprehensive Business Portfolio

## Part 2: Livestock Trading & Genetics Business Opportunities

---

## Executive Summary

Beyond milk production, livestock trading represents a significant revenue opportunity for Smart Dairy. With Bangladesh's growing demand for quality dairy animals, beef cattle, and breeding stock, Smart Dairy can leverage its expertise, infrastructure, and reputation to become a leading player in the livestock trading ecosystem.

**Market Context:**
- Bangladesh has 23.1 million cattle (DLS 2012 data)
- Only 6 million are dairy cattle, with 85-90% being indigenous low-yield breeds
- Massive demand for improved genetics and high-yield animals
- Growing beef market with shifting preferences toward quality meat
