# Smart Dairy Ltd. - Comprehensive Business Portfolio

## Part 1: Dairy Products Business Opportunities

---

## Executive Summary

Smart Dairy Ltd., a subsidiary of Smart Group, currently operates as a technology-driven organic dairy farm with 255 head of cattle producing 900 liters of milk daily. This document explores comprehensive dairy product business opportunities that Smart Dairy can pursue to expand from its current product line (Saffron brand) and capture a larger share of Bangladesh's growing dairy market.

**Current Status (2026):**
| Parameter | Current | 2-Year Target |
|-----------|---------|---------------|
| Total Cattle | 255 | 800 |
| Daily Milk Production | 900 liters | 3,000 liters |
| Lactating Cows | 75 | ~250 |
