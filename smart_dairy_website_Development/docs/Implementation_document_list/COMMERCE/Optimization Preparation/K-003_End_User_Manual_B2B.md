## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Welcome to Smart Dairy B2B

Welcome to the Smart Dairy Business-to-Business (B2B) Marketplace Portal! This platform is designed specifically for businesses like restaurants, cafes, retail shops, institutional buyers, and distributors to purchase quality dairy products at competitive prices with convenient ordering options.

**What You Can Do:**
- Browse business-only product catalog with tiered pricing
- Place bulk orders with volume discounts
- Use Quick Order for fast reordering
- Upload orders via CSV for large volumes
- Manage credit limits and payment terms
- Create standing orders for regular deliveries
- Track all orders in real-time
- Download and manage invoices
- Access detailed business reports and analytics

### 1.2 System Requirements

**For Web Browser:**
- Chrome 90+ or Firefox 88+ or Safari 14+ or Edge 90+
- JavaScript enabled
- Screen resolution: 1366x768 or higher
- Stable internet connection (broadband recommended)

**For Mobile App:**
- iOS 13.0+ or Android 8.0+
- 100MB free storage space
- Internet connection (Wi-Fi or 4G/LTE)

### 1.3 Business Types Supported

| Business Type | Examples | Features |
|-------------|-----------|----------|
| **Restaurant** | Fine dining, casual dining, fast food | Standing orders, priority delivery |
| **Cafe/Bakery** | Coffee shops, bakeries | Fresh products, morning delivery |
| **Retail Shop** | Grocery stores, mini-markets | Bulk pricing, flexible scheduling |
| **Institutional** | Hotels, hospitals, schools | Credit terms, monthly billing |
| **Distributor** | Wholesalers, sub-distributors | Volume pricing, multiple locations |

## 2. Getting Started

### 2.1 Initial Registration

#### 2.1.1 Business Registration

**Step-by-Step Process:**

1. **Visit B2B Portal**
   - Go to: https://b2b.smartdairy.com.bd
   - Click "Register Your Business" button

2. **Business Information Form**
   ```
   Required Fields:
   - Company Name: Legal business name
   - Trade License: Trade license number
   - Business Type: Select from dropdown (Restaurant, Cafe, Retail Shop, etc.)
   - Industry Sector: F&B, Retail, etc.
   - Years in Operation: Number of years
   - Estimated Monthly Purchases: BDT amount
   
   Contact Information:
   - Business Owner Name: Primary contact person
   - Designated Contact: Person for orders/deliveries
   - Mobile Number: Bangladesh format (017XXXXXXXX)
   - Email Address: Business email
   - Office Phone: Landline number (optional)
   
   Address Information:
   - Registered Office Address: Billing address
   - Delivery Address: Multiple delivery locations supported
   ```

3. **Upload Documents (Required for Verification)**
   - Trade License (PDF or image)
   - Company Registration Certificate
   - Tax Identification Number (TIN) Certificate
   - Business Owner NID (front and back)
   - Bank Account Details (for credit setup)

4. **Review and Submit**
   - Review all information
   - Accept Terms & Conditions
   - Accept Privacy Policy
   - Click "Submit Registration"

5. **Verification Process**
   - Initial review: 1-2 business days
   - Document verification: 1-3 business days
   - Credit assessment: 3-5 business days
   - Approval notification: SMS and email

## 3. Registration & Verification

### 3.1 Verification Requirements

#### 3.1.1 Required Documents

| Document | Format | Purpose | Where to Upload |
|----------|--------|---------|-----------------|
| **Trade License** | PDF/JPG | Legal business operation | Registration form |
| **Company Registration** | PDF | Business entity verification | Registration form |
| **TIN Certificate** | PDF | Tax identification | Registration form |
| **NID of Owner** | JPG/PNG | Identity verification | Registration form |
| **Bank Account Details** | N/A | Credit account setup | Settings later |

#### 3.1.2 Document Upload Guidelines

**File Specifications:**
- PDF files: Maximum 5MB
- Images: JPG/PNG, maximum 2MB each
- Maximum 10 uploads per registration
- Files must be clear and legible

**Tips:**
- Scan documents in color if possible
- Ensure all text is readable
- Use minimum 300 DPI for scans
- Crop documents to remove borders

### 3.2 Credit Limit Application

#### 3.2.1 Credit Assessment Process

**Smart Dairy Credit Evaluation:**

| Factor | Weight | Description |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| **Business Age** | 20% | Longer operation = higher credit |
| **Monthly Purchase Volume** | 25% | Higher volume = higher credit |
| **Payment History** | 30% | On-time payments = higher credit |
| **Financial Statements** | 25% | Healthy finances = higher credit |

**Credit Limit Ranges:**
- New businesses: BDT 10,000 - 50,000
- 6-12 months history: BDT 50,000 - 200,000
- 1-2 years history: BDT 100,000 - 500,000
- 2+ years history: BDT 200,000 - 2,000,000
- Large enterprises: Custom credit limits

#### 3.2.2 Payment Terms

| Payment Term | Description | Eligibility |
|---------------|-------------|--------------|
| **Cash on Delivery (COD)** | Pay on delivery | All tiers |
| **15 Days Credit** | Net 15 days | Starter, Professional |
| **30 Days Credit** | Net 30 days | Professional, Enterprise |
| **45 Days Credit** | Net 45 days | Enterprise, Corporate |
| **60 Days Credit** | Net 60 days | Corporate only |

**Credit Fee Structure:**
- Interest on overdue amounts: 1.5% per month
- Late payment fee: BDT 500 after 7 days
- Credit review: Every 6 months

## 5. Quick Order

### 5.1 Quick Order Overview

**Quick Order Features:**
- Save frequently ordered products
- One-click reordering
- Adjust quantities easily
- Add multiple saved items to cart
- Access from dashboard for fast ordering

### 5.2 Setting Up Quick Order

#### 5.2.1 Adding Products to Quick Order

**Steps:**
1. Browse products or go to order history
2. Click "Add to Quick Order" on product
3. Set preferred quantity:
   ```
   Quick Order Setup:
   - Product: Fresh Milk 500ml
   - Preferred Quantity: 200 units
   - Minimum Order: 100 units (1 carton)
   - Pack Preference: Carton of 100
   ```
4. Add to Quick Order list

**Quick Order Limits:**
- Maximum 50 products in Quick Order
- Each product must have minimum quantity specified
- Quantities must be in pack multiples (e.g., 100, 200 for carton of 100)

#### 5.2.2 Managing Quick Order Items

**Actions Available:**
- **Edit Quantity:** Change ordered quantity
- **Remove Item:** Remove from Quick Order
- **Move to Wishlist:** Keep but not in Quick Order
- **Duplicate:** Copy item with different quantity

### 5.3 Placing Quick Order

**Quick Order Process:**

1. Go to "Quick Order" from dashboard
2. Review all saved items and quantities
3. Add to cart with one click
4. Proceed to checkout
5. Select delivery address and time
6. Review order and confirm

**Benefits:**
- Saves time on repeat orders
- Ensures consistent quantities
- Easy to adjust for seasonal variations
- Reduces ordering errors

## 6. Bulk Order Management

### 6.1 CSV Order Upload

#### 6.1.1 CSV Upload Feature

**Purpose:** Upload orders for large quantities without manual entry

**CSV Template:**
```csv
Product SKU,Quantity,Unit Price Override (Optional)
SD-MILK-500,1000,
SD-YOG-500,500,
SD-CHD-2000,25,
```

**CSV Upload Process:**
1. Download CSV template from portal
2. Fill in your order details
3. Save CSV file
4. Click "Upload CSV Order"
5. Select file from computer
6. System validates CSV
7. Review order summary
8. Add to cart

**CSV Specifications:**
- Maximum 500 items per CSV
- File size limit: 5MB
- Must use provided template
- SKU codes must be valid

#### 6.1.2 CSV Validation

**System Checks:**
- SKU code validity: Product must exist in catalog
- Quantity validation: Must meet minimum order requirements
- Price override: Only allowed for negotiated contracts
- Duplicate prevention: Same SKU cannot appear twice

**Error Handling:**
- Invalid SKUs marked in red with error message
- Quantity below minimum: Shows required minimum
- Format errors: Highlighted with line number

### 6.2 Bulk Order Features

#### 6.2.1 Mixed Carton Orders

**Create Custom Carton:**
1. Browse products within same category
2. Click "Create Custom Carton"
3. Select products and quantities:
   ```
   Custom Carton Example (Milk Category):
   - Fresh Cow Milk 500ml: 50 units
   - Chocolate Milk 500ml: 30 units
   - Strawberry Milk 500ml: 20 units
   - Total: 100 units
   ```
4. Name your custom carton
5. Save for future reordering

## 17. FAQ

### 17.1 General Questions

**Q1: How do I register as a business customer?**
A: Go to b2b.smartdairy.com.bd and click "Register Your Business". Complete the registration form with your business details, upload required documents, and submit for verification.

**Q2: What credit limits are available?**
A: Credit limits range from BDT 10,000 to BDT 2,000,000+ based on your business type, purchase volume, and payment history. Higher tiers offer larger credit limits and better payment terms.

**Q3: What payment terms are available?**
A: We offer Cash on Delivery, 15 Days, 30 Days, 45 Days, and 60 Days credit terms, depending on your account tier and credit history.

**Q4: Can I order from multiple delivery locations?**
A: Yes! You can add up to 10 delivery locations to your account and select the appropriate location for each order.

**Q5: What are the minimum order requirements?**
A: Minimum order requirements vary by product and pack size. Individual items typically require minimum 100 units (1 carton). Check product page for specific minimums.

### 17.2 Ordering Questions

**Q6: What is Quick Order and how does it work?**
A: Quick Order allows you to save frequently ordered products for one-click reordering. Add products from catalog or order history to your Quick Order list, then add all items to cart with a single click.

**Q7: Can I upload orders via CSV?**
A: Yes! You can upload up to 500 items per CSV order. Download the template from the portal, fill in your order, and upload for processing.

**Q8: What are standing orders?**
A: Standing orders are recurring orders that are automatically generated according to your schedule (daily, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly). They ensure reliable delivery and automatic ordering.

**Q9: Can I modify or cancel standing orders?**
A: Yes! You can edit quantities, change delivery schedules, pause orders for specific periods, or cancel standing orders entirely from the Standing Orders dashboard.

**Q10: How do volume discounts work?**
A: Volume discounts are automatically applied based on your monthly purchase volume. The higher your volume, the greater the discount (5% to 25% off retail prices).

### 17.3 Payment Questions

**Q11: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: We accept Bank Transfer, bKash, Nagad, Checks, and Auto-Debit for business customers. The available methods depend on your account tier.

**Q12: Can I pay multiple invoices at once?**
A: Yes! From the Invoice Dashboard, you can select multiple invoices and pay them all at once with a single payment.

**Q13: What happens if I miss a payment due date?**
A: A late fee of BDT 500 is applied after 7 days past due date. Interest of 1.5% per month is charged on overdue amounts. Repeated late payments may affect your credit limit.

**Q14: How can I increase my credit limit?**
A: Credit limit reviews are conducted every 6 months. If your payment history is good and purchase volume has increased, you can request a credit limit increase from your account settings or by contacting the sales team.

**Q15: Can I get a statement of my credit balance and transactions?**
A: Yes! From the Credit Management dashboard, you can view your credit balance, payment history, and download monthly statements in PDF format.

### 17.4 Delivery Questions

**Q16: What are the delivery charges for business customers?**
A: Delivery charges vary by order size and location. Standard delivery is BDT 50 per location, Express is BDT 100, and Scheduled is BDT 70. Multi-location orders incur the standard fee per location.

**Q17: Can I choose a specific delivery time?**
A: Yes! You can select from available time slots (Morning, Midday, Afternoon, Evening) during order placement or configure preferred times in your location settings.

**Q18: What if I need an order urgently (same day)?**
A: Express delivery is available for BDT 100 per location and must be placed before 2 PM for same-day delivery.

**Q19: Can I pick up my order instead of delivery?**
A: Yes! Store pickup is free. Select "Pickup" as the delivery option during order placement and choose a convenient pickup time.

**Q20: What if I'm not at the delivery location when it arrives?**
A: The delivery agent will attempt to contact the designated contact person. If unreachable, the agent will leave a contact notice and return the order to the Smart Dairy facility for redelivery.

### 17.5 Account Questions

**Q21: How do I update my business information?**
A: Go to "My Account" â†’ "Business Profile" and update your information. Changes to some fields (like Trade License) may require additional verification and documentation.

**Q22: Can I add multiple users to my business account?**
A: Yes! Owners and managers can add up to 20 additional users with different permission levels (Manager, Staff, Viewer). Each user has their own login credentials.

**Q23: How can I add or remove delivery locations?**
A: From "My Account" â†’ "Locations", you can add up to 10 delivery locations. Each location must be verified by Smart Dairy. You can also edit or remove locations from this page.

**Q24: How do I change my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page or go to "My Account" â†’ "Security" to change your password. You'll need to verify your identity with an OTP.

**Q25: Can I close my business account?**
A: To close your business account, all outstanding balances must be paid and all standing orders cancelled. Contact B2B support to initiate the closure process.
