## 2. BRAND IDENTITY

### 2.1 Brand Values

| Value | Definition | Digital Expression |
|-------|------------|-------------------|
| **Quality First** | Commitment to 100% pure organic milk | Clean, premium design; quality certifications prominently displayed |
| **Transparency** | Open about our farming practices | Farm visibility, traceability features, authentic photography |
| **Innovation** | Technology-driven dairy farming | Modern UI, smart features, IoT integration showcase |
| **Sustainability** | Environmentally responsible | Green messaging, carbon footprint tracking, biogas highlights |
| **Community** | Supporting Bangladeshi farmers | Farmer stories, community impact metrics, local partnerships |

### 2.2 Brand Positioning Statement

**For** health-conscious families and businesses in Bangladesh
**Who** want safe, organic dairy products they can trust
**Smart Dairy** is a premium dairy brand
**That** combines traditional farming values with modern technology
**Unlike** conventional dairy suppliers
**We** provide complete farm-to-table traceability and guaranteed organic quality
