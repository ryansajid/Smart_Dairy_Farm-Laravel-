## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Project Overview

Smart Dairy Ltd., a subsidiary of Smart Group, is seeking proposals from qualified web development agencies for the design, development, and deployment of a **world-class public website and corporate portal**. This digital platform will serve as the primary touchpoint for customers, investors, partners, and stakeholders to engage with the Smart Dairy brand.

### 1.2 Business Context

Smart Dairy operates at the intersection of traditional dairy farming and modern agricultural technology. With a current herd of 255 cattle producing 900 liters of milk daily, and ambitious plans to scale to 800 cattle producing 3,000 liters daily within 2 years, Smart Dairy requires a digital presence that reflects its commitment to:

- **Quality**: 100% organic, antibiotic-free dairy products
- **Innovation**: Technology-driven farming practices
- **Sustainability**: Biogas plants, organic farming, environmental stewardship
- **Transparency**: Farm-to-consumer traceability
- **Growth**: Aggressive expansion and market leadership

### 1.3 Key Objectives

| Objective | Description | Success Metric |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| **Brand Positioning** | Establish Smart Dairy as Bangladesh's premier organic dairy brand | Brand recognition survey |
| **Customer Engagement** | Create immersive digital experiences that educate and engage visitors | Average session duration >3 minutes |
| **Lead Generation** | Capture B2B leads, franchise inquiries, and partnership opportunities | 100+ qualified leads/month |
| **Investor Relations** | Showcase company growth, sustainability initiatives, and financial health | Investor inquiry conversion rate |
| **Recruitment** | Attract top talent through compelling employer branding | Job application rate |
| **Information Hub** | Serve as authoritative source for dairy industry information | Return visitor rate >40% |

### 1.4 Target Audience

| Audience Segment | Demographics | Needs & Expectations |
|-----------------|--------------|---------------------|
| **B2C Consumers** | Urban families, health-conscious individuals, ages 25-55 | Product information, quality assurance, purchase locations, health benefits |
| **B2B Buyers** | Retailers, restaurants, hotels, institutional buyers | Wholesale information, quality certifications, supply chain reliability |
| **Investors** | Domestic and international investors, venture capitalists | Company performance, growth strategy, ESG initiatives |
| **Job Seekers** | Agricultural professionals, veterinarians, tech talent | Career opportunities, company culture, growth prospects |

| **Farmers & Partners** | Dairy farmers, agricultural suppliers, technology partners | Partnership opportunities, knowledge sharing, best practices |

## 6. Technical Requirements & Architecture

### 6.1 Technology Stack Recommendations

Based on industry best practices and Smart Dairy's requirements, the following technology stack is recommended:

#### 6.1.1 Recommended CMS Platforms

| Platform | Pros | Cons | Best For |
|----------|------|------|----------|
| **WordPress** | Extensive ecosystem, easy to use, cost-effective | Security concerns at scale, performance optimization needed | Small to medium sites, content-heavy |
| **Drupal** | Enterprise-grade security, highly scalable, robust | Steeper learning curve, higher development cost | Large enterprises, complex requirements |
| **Laravel + Custom CMS** | Fully customizable, high performance, secure | Higher development cost, longer timeline | Custom requirements, high traffic |
| **Next.js + Headless CMS** | Modern architecture, excellent performance, flexible | Requires JavaScript expertise, API dependencies | Modern web apps, omnichannel |

**Recommendation:** Laravel-based custom solution or Next.js with headless CMS for optimal performance, security, and customization.

#### 6.1.2 Frontend Technologies

| Technology | Purpose | Justification |
|------------|---------|---------------|
| **HTML5/CSS3** | Structure and styling | Modern standards, semantic markup |
| **JavaScript/TypeScript** | Interactivity | Type safety, better developer experience |
| **React.js or Vue.js** | Component framework | Reusable components, virtual DOM performance |
| **Tailwind CSS** | Styling framework | Utility-first, rapid development, consistent design |
