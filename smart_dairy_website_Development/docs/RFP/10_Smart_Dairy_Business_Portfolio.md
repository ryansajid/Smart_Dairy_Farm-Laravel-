The vision emerged from Smart Group Chairman Mohd. Mazharul Islam's personal commitment to contributing to national food security.

## 8.2 Biogas Plant Details

**Biogas Utilization:**
- 67% Electricity Generation (100 kWh/day)
- 16% Thermal Use - Milk Processing
- 12% Staff Kitchen Cooking
- 5% Water Heating
