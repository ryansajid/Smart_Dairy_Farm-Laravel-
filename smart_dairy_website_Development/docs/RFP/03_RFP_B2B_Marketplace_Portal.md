## 1. Executive Summary

### 1.1 Project Overview

Smart Dairy Ltd., a leading organic dairy farming enterprise in Bangladesh and subsidiary of the diversified Smart Group, seeks to develop and deploy a comprehensive **B2B Marketplace Portal** to revolutionize wholesale dairy and agricultural product distribution in Bangladesh.

This RFP invites qualified technology vendors and development partners to submit comprehensive proposals for the design, development, implementation, and ongoing support of an enterprise-grade B2B e-commerce platform tailored specifically for the dairy and organic food industry.

### 1.2 Business Context

The dairy industry in Bangladesh is experiencing rapid transformation driven by:

- **Growing demand for organic products**: Consumers increasingly prefer antibiotic-free, preservative-free dairy products
- **Supply chain fragmentation**: Traditional distribution channels are inefficient and lack transparency
- **Digital transformation**: B2B buyers expect modern, seamless digital purchasing experiences
- **Scale requirements**: Smart Dairy's planned expansion from 900 liters/day to 3,000 liters/day requires robust distribution infrastructure
- **Market opportunity**: Significant untapped potential in B2B segments including HORECA (Hotels, Restaurants, Catering), retail chains, institutions, and corporate clients

### 1.3 Project Objectives

| Objective | Description | Success Metrics |
|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Revenue Growth** | Establish direct B2B sales channel reducing dependency on traditional distributors | 30% revenue increase within 12 months of launch |
| **Operational Efficiency** | Automate order processing, inventory management, and partner onboarding | 50% reduction in order processing time |
| **Partner Network** | Build scalable partner ecosystem supporting 500+ B2B customers within first year | 500+ active B2B partners by end of Year 1 |
| **Market Expansion** | Enable geographic expansion beyond current Dhaka-centric operations | Presence in 5+ major cities within 18 months |
| **Customer Experience** | Provide 24/7 self-service ordering with personalized pricing and catalogs | NPS score >50, <2 hour response time |
| **Data Insights** | Establish analytics-driven decision making for sales and operations | Real-time dashboards with 50+ KPIs |
| **Integration Excellence** | Seamless integration with existing ERP, accounting, and logistics systems | 99.9% data synchronization accuracy |
